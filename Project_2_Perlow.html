Welcome to Russ' Zombie Shooter

How To Play

Zombies are at it again! As always, they want your brains. Avoid them by moving around and shoot to kill! Don't worry, the map is endless. Do whatever it takes to keep yourself alive as long as possible! Good Luck!

Controls:

  • WASD to move
  • Esc to pause/unpause
  • Left Click to Shoot
  • R to reload
  • Move mouse to rotate direction
  • Enter to begin playing
